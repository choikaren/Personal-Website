Karen is high school senior in New York City. She hopes to pursue architecture in the future. She is currently an Interior Design Assistant at American & International Designs and has previously interned at Designer's Edge and Stantec Architecture and Engineering.
Karen loves seeing ideas and concepts transform and come to life. In her free time, she loves drawing, painting, learning how to code, and studying architecture. She also enjoys painting murals around the city addressing socio-political concerns. She hopes to be an ambassodor for inclusion and an advocate for improving contemporary issues. Karen is a co-founder of Warbler Org, an environmental organization with a goal of combating environmental and animal injustices, and striving for equity among racial and ethic minorities. She is also currently the Design Director at DetesterMagazine and hopes to impact others by speaking out about gender equality and environmental justice. She recognizes the need for sustainable design and hopes to explore the field of eco-friendly architecture in college.
