A Long Ride
2020
Acrylic on Canvas 12x16
On the 2 train travelling through Manhattan.
Art is about more than just the visual representation or outcome; art is about the journey the artist encounters while working on the piece. It’s a personal channel in which the dancer, musician, painter may respectively dance, play, paint their hearts out to escape from the real world. It’s an individual outlet where they can run from their struggles, their pains and lose track of time loving what they do, doing what they love. It’s more of a train ride than anything - boldly confronting their internal struggles in the midst of a journey that culminates to only a final product the public may see. Art truly is more than what the surface is able to capture; it’s a whirlwind focusing on the trek, rather the destination at which they arrive at. And, the phenomenal thing is that it’s unique to everyone’s independent journey and means something different to all of us because we all perceive the world differently.
Karen Choi 2020
